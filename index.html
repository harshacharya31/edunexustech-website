Digital Solutions for Schools

Streamline library operations, improve student engagement, and gain actionable insights through a modern Library Management System designed specifically for schools.

Key Features

📚 Books Management

  • Centralized digital catalog for all books.
  • Search books by title, author, category.
  • Real-time visibility of available and issued books.

🔄 Issue & Return Management

  • Quick and efficient issue/return process.
  • Track due dates and overdue books.
  • Maintain complete borrowing history.

👨‍🎓 Student Management

  • Manage student records and memberships.
  • Track issued books and reading history.
  • Monitor active and inactive library members.

📈 Analytics & Reports

  • Identify popular and high-demand books.
  • Track library utilization trends.
  • Generate insights for budgeting and planning.
